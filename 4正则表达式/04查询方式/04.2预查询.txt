1  预查询(正向预查,正预测,前瞻,先行断言)   (?=ing\b) 是ing就不查了,到ing停止
    var str = 'hello, when i am working,do not coming';
    var reg = /\b\w+(?=ing\b)/g;
    var m = str.match(reg);
    console.log(m); //Array [ "work", "com" ]

2  负向预查(负预测,前瞻,现行否定)   (?!ing)\w{3}\b  \w{3}\b 后边3位  (?!ing) 不是ing
    var str = 'hello, when i am working,do not coming';
    var reg = /\b\w+(?!ing)\w{3}\b/g;
    var m = str.match(reg);
    console.log(m); //Array [ "hello", "when" ]
